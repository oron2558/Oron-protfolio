How It Works

Step 01
Watch the Ball
A virtual ball flies toward you on the 3D court. Track its trajectory.
Step 02
Time Your Swing
Press SPACE (or HIT button) when the green ring appears. Timing is everything.
Step 03
See the Result
Perfect timing → clean winner. Too early/late → net or miss.
Step 04
Add Real Sensor
Attach an ESP32 sensor to your racket. The system reads your actual swing. (Phase 2)
